Our Services

Comprehensive evaluation and documentation services to support your VA disability claim.

📝

DBQ Evaluations

A Disability Benefits Questionnaire (DBQ) is a standardized form used by the VA to evaluate your medical conditions. Our licensed medical examiners conduct thorough examinations and complete these forms with the detail and accuracy the VA requires.

We cover all condition categories including mental health, musculoskeletal, neurological, and more. Each evaluation is tailored to your specific conditions and service history.

🔗

Nexus Letters

A nexus letter establishes the critical link between your current medical condition and your military service. This medical opinion from a qualified professional is often the deciding factor in whether a VA claim is approved or denied.

Our nexus letters are written by licensed professionals who understand what the VA considers persuasive medical evidence. We review your complete medical and service history to build a strong, evidence-based connection.

📂

Medical Record Review

Before your evaluation, our team conducts a comprehensive review of your medical records and service history. This helps us identify all potentially service-connected conditions — including those you may not have considered.

This preliminary review ensures your evaluation is thorough and that no conditions are overlooked in the documentation process.

🧭

Claims Guidance

Navigating the VA disability claims process can be overwhelming. We provide personalized guidance at every step — from understanding your eligibility to preparing for your evaluation and knowing what to expect after submission.

While we are not attorneys and do not file claims on your behalf, we ensure your medical documentation is complete and properly prepared for submission.
