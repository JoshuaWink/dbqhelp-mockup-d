About DBQ Help

Veteran disability evaluations by former VA professionals who know what the VA needs to see.

Our Mission

Empowering Veterans to Secure the Benefits They've Earned

DBQ Help was founded with a single purpose: to ensure that every veteran who served this country has their service-connected conditions properly documented. Too many veterans struggle with the VA claims process — not because their conditions aren't real, but because the documentation doesn't meet the VA's specific requirements.

Our team includes former VA raters who spent years reviewing disability claims from the other side of the desk. They know exactly what the VA needs to see in a DBQ evaluation, what makes a nexus letter persuasive, and what documentation gaps cause claims to be denied.

We combine this insider knowledge with compassionate, thorough medical evaluations conducted by licensed professionals. The result: documentation that accurately reflects your conditions and meets the VA's standards.
